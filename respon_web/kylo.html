Kylo's Story

Kylo was found on the streets when he was just a kitten. My partner's extended family took him in before eventually giving it to my partner's direct family. Kylo was named after Kylo Ren, however there is zero simularities between personalities. Kylo lives with Nubs, and they have a love/hate relationship.
